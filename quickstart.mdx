## Get started in three steps

From install to your first hedge in just a few clicks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get access">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Get the extension" icon="download">
        Install the Polystrat extension and connect it to Polymarket.

        <Card title="Chrome Web  Store" icon="download" href="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/polystrat/bljdmoemdghfaikkjabeajhmplgkddgn?hl=en-US" cta="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/polystrat/">
          
        </Card>
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Apply your invite code" icon="user-check">
        Unlock access and activate all features with your invite code.

        <Note>
          Don't have one? [Apply for early access](https://www.polystr.at/).
        </Note>
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Connect your Polymarket's Magic Wallet" icon="plug">
        Go to [polymarket.com/settings](https://polymarket.com/settings)\
        Click “Export Private Key”\
        Paste it into the extension to authorize Polystrat

        <Warning>
          **We’re temporarily supporting Magic Wallet only.**\
          If your Polymarket account was created using an external wallet, Magic keys can’t be exported. In that case, please [create a new Polymarket account](https://polymarket.com/) using email to continue.
        </Warning>
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your first hedge">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="BTC/ETH markets" icon="bitcoin-sign">
        Use Polystrat’s statistical model to structure hedged entries on crypto markets.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Other markets" icon="money-bill-wave">
        Split your entry across multiple outcomes to improve coverage and winning chances.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite and earn">
    <Accordion title="Set your referral code" icon="share">
      Invite others to Polystrat and [earn from their activity.](https://docs.polystr.at/polystrat/referral)
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
</Steps>
